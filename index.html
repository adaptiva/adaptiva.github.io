Our Services

Product Development

Our network of experts has extensive experience of all aspects of product delivery from conceptualisation of a product right through to supporting and maintaining your product.

Strategy

We have a strong leadership team and access to leading strategy consultants that can help shape your product delivery model, development departments, and the general direction that you want to head in. We can help you avoid some of the pitfalls that your software project or company could face on the road to success.

Training

Experience tells us that a well drilled and skilled workforce is an essential ingredient in any successful software project or company. For this reason we have invested time and skill to provide training and workshops in agile, lean, and continuous software delivery, key software engineering skills, product management, and quality management

Due Diligence

If you are buying or selling a business and want to know more about the key aspects of the technical, developemnt or engineering departments, we can give you the insight you need to make the right decision about a sale or purchase. We specialise in working with venture capitalists to provide expert guidance on the true cost of ownership of particular investments that have a strong technical or software related nature.
